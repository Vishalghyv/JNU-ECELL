IRADA
Dr. Akshansh Gupta has completed his Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) from Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), despite being 95 per cent disabled. He suffers from Cerebral Palsy and fought many odds for five years to complete his thesis on ‘Brain Computer Interface’ in the School of Computer and System Sciences, JNU. Despite having an M.Tech and PhD, he finds it hard to get employment, so he decided to do it on his own. He founded a company and now actively working along with two fellow PhD scholars from JNU, K R Anandam and Prabhat Ranjan. The company, called IRADA (Integrative Research Analysis and Development Academy), aims to promote education and research for the school students as well as PhD scholars.” It provides career counselling at each stage of education. The venture also guides young and novice researchers to how to pursue their research and write a research paper.

CollCom
Mr. Gaurav Kumar is a Senior Research Fellow, pursuing his Ph.D. in Computer Science from School of Computer & Systems Sciences, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. He is a very passionate social activist and working towards creating a behavioural transformation through community service. He has founded an organization, named CollCom (College-Community), to provide a platform for all colleges and +2 schools' students to work closely with community/villages such as street children, migrated people, slums, orphanage, old age, safari-karamchari, divyang, drugs abused people, jail inmates, and farmers to understand their problems and need, and involve themselves in the problem solving process, so that life of these people or villagers can be raised at higher material and moral level.
